SEAS Executive Education: Applying LLMs in Practice
| 2025 Spring

APCOMP 209B: Advanced Topics in Data Science
| 2024, 2025 Spring

Topics included clustering algorithms, Bayesian inference, mixture models, MLPs, CNNs, RNNs, GRUs/LSTMs, embeddings, autoencoders, GANs, transformers, BERT, GPT, and large language modeling.

APCOMP 209A: Introduction to Data Science
| 2023, 2024, 2025 Fall

Topics included exploratory data analysis & cleaning, web scraping, visualization, \(k\)-NN & linear regression, classification & logistic regression, decision trees & ensemble methods, dimensionality reduction, imputation, and causal inference.

STAT 139: Introduction to Linear Models
| 2023 Fall

Topics included hypothesis testing (\(z\), \(t\), ANOVA), bootstrapping, linear, multilinear, & polynomial regression, smoothers, model & variable selection, regularization, and basics of generalized linear models.

CSCI 7: Introduction to Computer Science

Topics included operators & data types in Python, control flow, functions, object oriented programming, exception handling, file handling, using APIs, big \(O\) notation & time complexity, recursion, and sorting algorithms.
